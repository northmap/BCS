Dear User,
Welcome to the Binevenagh and Coastal Lowlands Interactive Map. This map was created to increase awareness of the archaeology within the Binevenagh and Magilligan areas.

This map was developed by Causeway Coast and Glens Heritage Trust as part of the Recording and Monitoring Magilligan's Eroding Coastal Archaeology project funded by the Department for Communities: Historic Environment Division through their Historic Environment Fund Research stream. Through this project CCGHT is working with volunteers to record and monitor archaeological sites along the Lough Foyle shoreline of Magilligan that are at risk from coastal erosion. We cannot protect and preserve all these sites, but by recording and monitoring them on a regular basis we can pinpoint their locations and extents, note the nature of their contained materials and highlight those worthy of further detailed research before they are ultimately destroyed.

The map also gives an overview of archaeological and historic projects completed in the Binevenagh area as part of the Binevenagh and Coastal Lowlands Landscape Partnership Scheme and funded by the National Lottery Heritage Fund.

The map will be updated with new information when necessary.

Instructions
Sites of interest appearing in the map are listed in the side menu. They have been categorised into the following three themes:
• Volunteer Survey Data: The information/photos collected by volunteers along the Magilligan Shoreline.
• Historic Environment Projects: Ongoing historic and archaeology projects in the area.
• NI Sites and Monuments Record: Details of archaeological sites in the area as recorded by DfC: Historic Environment Division.

Click on the 'Themes' button, select your preferences and the menu and map will filter the sites accordingly. Click a menu item or map point to zoom to its location and reveal further information.
